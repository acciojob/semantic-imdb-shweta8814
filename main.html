Actor 1: Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is an acclaimed American actor known for his roles in "Inception", "The Revenant", and "Titanic". He has won numerous awards and is celebrated for his diverse acting skills.

Actor 2: Meryl Streep

Meryl Streep is an American actress widely regarded as one of the greatest actresses of her generation. Her notable films include "The Devil Wears Prada", "Sophie's Choice", and "The Iron Lady".

Actor 3: Morgan Freeman

Morgan Freeman is a renowned American actor known for his distinctive voice and roles in films like "The Shawshank Redemption", "Million Dollar Baby", and "Se7en".
