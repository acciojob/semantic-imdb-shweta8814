Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for implanting an idea into a target’s subconscious—known as inception. The movie explores complex themes of reality, dreams, and the mind as Cobb assembles a team to complete the task and faces numerous challenges along the way.
